The Goal: Scientific Revolution Part II

Speaking technically, this project aims to get external data into a blockchain reliably, and use that data efficiently and conservatively.

However, unlike other blockchain-projects, the ultimate purpose is NOT to establish a new payment system (nor to delude the innocent with get-rich-quick nonsense), but instead to...

...revolutionize the way information is created and spread, giving the internet a "frontal lobe"... ...overhaul democratic governance (highlighting the irrelevance of showmanship/PR/race/gender/who-candidate-has-slept-with)... ...boost economic growth... ...cut out all Wall St. / banking middlemen and replace them with tech... ...spearhead the campaign to make Bitcoin the world's internet currency... ...and more!
